Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 7 Dec 2024

This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle user information in our application, LitRPG Library ("App").

What Data We Collect

We collect and store minimal personal data. When you use Google Sign In, we receive and store the following information:

  • Your email address associated with your Google account
  • Your Google profile name

We do not receive or store your Google Account password.

How We Use Your Data

We use the information collected to:

  • Create and manage your account on LitRPG Library
  • Display your profile name in relevant sections of the App
  • Communicate with you about your account and our services

Third-Party Services

LitRPG Library uses Google Sign In for authentication. When you use this service, your email address and profile name are processed by Google. For more information on how Google uses your data, please visit Google's Privacy Policy.

Data Security and Retention

Your email address and profile name are stored securely in our database. We retain this information for as long as you maintain an active account with us. You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time.

Your Rights and Choices

You can:

  • Choose not to use Google Sign In and create an account directly on LitRPG Library
  • Revoke our access to your Google account at any time through your Google Account settings
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data from LitRPG Library

Compliance

We comply with Google's API Services User Data Policy and applicable privacy laws. Our use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at nyxo59bt@students.codeinstitute.net.
